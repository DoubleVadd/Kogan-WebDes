Mattress Buying Guide

Mattress Buying Guide

The ultisaaste guide to choosing your mattress

When you go to buy a new mattress, choosing the correct mattress for your body is key to a perfect night’s sleep.

Ergolux delivers comfort and body support with its range of ergonomic mattresses.

These mattresses unlock greater breathability, support, and ‘just right’ firmness to help you sleep in restorative comfort.

What is a Memory Foam mattress?

Memory foam constantly moves with you to evenly distribute body weight in the mattress.

The memory foam in the mattress helps take the pressure off joints to ensure every individual part of your body is cushioned, supported, and comfortable.

Motion-absorbing properties make memory foam mattresses ideal for reducing partner disturbance by reducing motion transfer to other areas of the mattress.

A memory foam mattress with the right firmness for you can help to reduce back pains and can deliver pressure relief.

What is a Latex Foam mattress?

Latex mattresses offer firm support by moulding to the shape of your body.

The latex in the mattress helps to cushion and gently cradle pressure points like your hips and shoulders while encouraging proper spine alignment.

The open-cell structure of latex also allows for greater air circulation and breathability, helping to create a more optimal sleeping temperature.

Latex foam mattresses made from natural latex are eco-friendly and produce no harsh chemicals in the mattress.

What is a Pocket Spring mattress?

The pocket spring system in the mattress incorporates individually wrapped coil springs surrounded by comfort layers. Independent mattress springs distribute weight throughout the mattress by adapting to different pressure zones. Pocket spring mattresses are surrounded by a comfort foam layer to add the benefit of contouring foam.

What is a Bonnell Spring mattress?

The Bonnell spring coil system uses springs in the mattress that are hourglass shaped with an interconnected mesh of metal.

The wider hourglass spring shape provides durability and support by allowing weight to be evenly distributed across all zones of the mattress.

Firmness

The right mattress firmness will depend on the individual. Many factors influence which firmness is right for you, including age, weight, sleeping position age and personal preference. Mattresses should provide both comfort and support for a restful night’s sleep.

Mattresses on Kogan.com come in the following firmness options:

Plush

  • Super soft & flexible

  • Disperses body weight, cradles the hips & shoulders & reduces stress on back

  • Flexible surface moulds to your body’s contours

Medium

  • Balance between soft flexibility & firmness

  • Aligns your hips & shoulders

  • Conforms to your body’s natural shape while retaining a level of firmness

Firm

  • Rigid & resistant top player for greater resistance

  • Maintains a neutral alignment of spine, hips & shoulders

  • Keeps the body straight to promote a healthy posture

Sleeping position

Your sleeping position also affects which mattress is best for you. Here are common sleeping positions and the mattress that we recommend you choose.

Side sleeper

  • Best suited to soft to medium mattresses

  • Ideally has a mattress that moulds to hips & shoulders

  • A soft to medium mattress can provide relief & comfort to side sleepers

Back sleeper

  • Will find the most comfort & support with medium to firm mattresses

  • Suited to a mattress that distributes weight evenly

  • A medium to firm mattress can help alleviate back pain for back sleepers

Stomach sleeper

  • Medium-firm to firm mattresses are most suitable & comfortable

  • The best fit is a mattress that elevates the pelvis

  • A medium-firm to firm mattress prevents uncomfortable extension of lower back

Mattress sizes

Single: 92 x 188 cm

King Single: 107 x 203 cm

Double: 138 x 188 cm

Queen: 153 x 203 cm

King: 203 x 203 cm

How to set up your mattress

The mattress will be delivered to you in a long box. Follow these instructions to set up your mattress effectively:

Step 1

Lay the box onto a flat surface or bed frame and slowly pull the mattress out of the packaging.

Step 2

Carefully cut the outer bag with scissors and roll the mattress out onto a flat surface or bed frame.

Step 3

Allow the mattress to expand for 24 hours before use.
